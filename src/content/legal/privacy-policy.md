# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Veyra is a fitness tracking Progressive Web App that helps users track workouts, nutrition, and personal fitness goals.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your information.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

When you use Veyra, we may collect:

### Account Information

- Email address
- User account identifier
- Authentication information

### Fitness Data

- Workout plans
- Exercises
- Workout history
- Meal information
- Calories and macronutrients
- Fitness goals

### Technical Data

- Device information
- Browser information
- Application usage data

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

- Provide fitness tracking functionality
- Save and synchronize your progress
- Improve application performance
- Maintain account security

We do not sell your personal information.

---

## 4. Data Storage

Veyra uses Supabase for authentication and database services.

Your information is securely stored using Supabase infrastructure and PostgreSQL databases.

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

After deletion, your personal information will be removed from our systems unless retention is legally required.
